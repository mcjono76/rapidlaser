Our Services

Comprehensive laser cutting and fabrication solutions

Design Services

At Rapid Laser, we specialize in transforming your ideas and concepts into practical, manufacturable designs. Our experienced design team works closely with you to understand your vision and requirements.

  • Concept development and refinement
  • 3D modeling and visualization
  • Design optimization for manufacturing
  • Prototyping and testing support
  • Collaborative design process

CAD Drafting

Our professional CAD drafting services provide detailed technical drawings for your designs. We use the latest CAD software to ensure precision and accuracy in every project.

  • Detailed technical drawings
  • 2D and 3D CAD modeling
  • Assembly drawings and exploded views
  • Material specifications
  • Manufacturing-ready documentation

Manufacturing

We manufacture customers' products with precision and quality. From small batches to large production runs, we have the capabilities to meet your manufacturing needs.

  • Precision manufacturing processes
  • Quality control and inspection
  • Small to medium batch production
  • Assembly and finishing services
  • Timely delivery and project management

Laser Cutting

We provide precision laser cutting services for customers across a wide variety of thicknesses and products. Our state-of-the-art laser cutting equipment ensures accurate cuts every time.

  • Cutting up to 20mm thickness
  • Various materials: steel, stainless steel, aluminum
  • Complex shapes and patterns
  • Fast turnaround times
  • Precision and repeatability
  • Integration with our fabrication services

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Our sheet metal fabrication services cover medium engineering projects. We laser cut all the steel for our custom made sheet metal fabrication projects in Bayswater.

  • Custom sheet metal work
  • CNC folding and bending
  • Welding and assembly
  • Surface finishing
  • Powder coating preparation
  • Quality assurance
